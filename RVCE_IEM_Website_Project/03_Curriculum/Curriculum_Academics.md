## 3. Signature Lab / Experiential Progression (year-wise)
- **1st year — IDEA Lab:** hands-on IoT (sensors, actuators, devices).
- **2nd year — Design Thinking Lab:** structured ideation & innovation.
- **3rd year — Minor Project:** industry/research problem, applied solution.
- **Final year — Major Project:** project management + simulation + research to solve complex industrial problems; faculty encourage converting projects into conference/journal papers.

**Source:** https://rvce.edu.in/im-faculties · ✅

## 4. Knowledge Areas Covered
Mechanical Engineering fundamentals · Industrial Engineering · Functional management (Marketing, Finance, HR) · Information Systems · Supply Chain Management · Quality Management · Quantitative/decision models (OR, Statistics, Simulation, Optimization).

## 5. Internship / Capstone Requirements
Summer internship options (official): (a) in-house at RVCE department (~3 weeks), (b) at RVCE Centres of Excellence (~16 CoEs + 6 Centres of Competence), (c) via Internshala, (d) at hometown engineering colleges, (e) at industry/research orgs (BEL, DRDO, ISRO, BHEL) via formal acceptance letter. Final-year **Major Project** acts as capstone.
**Source:** https://rvce.edu.in/im-faculties · ✅

## 6. Evaluation Structure (CIE + SEE)
**2022 scheme (current):** Two quizzes (20) + two tests reduced to 40 + Experiential Learning 40 (Case study 10, Program-specific 10, Video seminar 10, Design/modeling 10) → **CIE 100**. Lab: exercises/report (20) + lab test (10) + innovative experiment (20) = **50**.
**2021 scheme:** Quizzes 20 + Tests→40 + EL (Phase I 20 + Phase II 20)=40; Lab 50.
**2018 scheme:** Quizzes 30 + Tests→50 + EL 20 (≥50% case studies); SEE 100 (Part-A objective 20 + Part-B 80); Lab CIE 50 / SEE 50.
**Source:** https://rvce.edu.in/im-faculties · ✅

## 7. Pedagogy & Teaching Innovations (official)
Active learning (Think-Pair-Share, Peer Instruction, Flipped Classroom, Interactive Lectures, PBL) · Integrated Experiential Learning (combining e.g. Marketing + Financial Accounting/Costing + Statistics with industry field visits) · Industry visits (2 per semester) · Expert lectures (≥1 per subject per semester) · NPTEL courses for credit · Webinars · Virtual labs · CAD/Simulation software · LMS (Google Classroom, Canvas) · Digital library · German university collaborations (4) for global exposure.
**Source:** https://rvce.edu.in/im-faculties · ✅
