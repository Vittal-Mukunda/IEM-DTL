> **How to read the "Basis" column — important.**
> The syllabus books **do not name instructors** (they carry only a generic "Faculty / Course Coordinator" line). So:
> - **✅ Official** = the teacher is taken from RVCE's published **course-coordinator / LMS mapping** (subject matched by name even where the 2021→2022 course code changed).
> - **◐ Best-fit** = *suggested* teacher inferred from the faculty member's **specialisation**; **confirm with the department** before treating as final.
> - **👥 All faculty** = projects/internships are guided by the whole department.

Only **IM-coded (Industrial Engineering & Management Board of Studies) courses** are listed — these are the *core* IEM subjects. Common/basket/institutional courses owned by other departments (MAT, CV, ME, BT, CS, HS, etc.) are excluded except where IEM offers them.

2. **◐ Best-fit** assignments are *suggestions* from specialisation — actual allotment changes each semester and **must be confirmed with the department / timetable**.
3. **Dr. Chitra B T (Law)** and **Dr. Bindu Ashwini C (Psychology)** handle Humanities/elective courses (IPR, Cyber Law, Applied/Cognitive Psychology, Universal Human Values) rather than IM-core subjects.
4. To finalise: request the **current semester course-allotment / timetable** from the department office — that is the only authoritative source for instructor-per-section.
