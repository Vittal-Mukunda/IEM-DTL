# PHASE 5 — STUDENT ACHIEVEMENTS
### Department of Industrial Engineering and Management, RVCE

| Date collected | 2026-06-10 |
|---|---|
| Verification | ⚠ Mostly department/institute level; IEM-specific student awards are not richly indexed publicly (GAP). |

## 1. Student body & association
- **IDEA — InDustrial Engineering Association**, the official department student association, drives curricular/co-curricular/extra-curricular activities and "holistic development of the department." Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/idearvce/ ✅
- Lead professional society: **Institute of Industrial Engineers (IIE)** student chapter. ✅

## 2. Cross-department flagship achievements involving IEM students
- **Project "Chimera"** — India's first indigenous hybrid-electric vehicle prototype (also using bio-diesel), developed by final-year students across **Mechanical, Electrical, Computer Science and Industrial Engineering** (reported by *The Hindu*, June 2008). ✅
**Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/R.V._College_of_Engineering

## 3. Institute-wide student platforms open to IEM students
Team Antariksh (student satellite, RVSAT-1) · StudSat (India's first pico-satellite, PSLV C-15, 2010) · Ashwa Racing (FSAE) · Team Vyoma (UAV) · Helios Racing (Baja ATV — national champion 2015) · Project Garuda (Shell Eco-Marathon) · CIRQuIT Quantum Research · IEEE/ACM student chapters. (Institute-level; many are open to all branches incl. IEM.) ✅
**Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/R.V._College_of_Engineering

## 4. Academic achievement culture (department)
- Faculty actively mentor final-year students to **convert Major Projects into conference/journal papers** (publication track record). ✅
- **NPTEL** certifications taken for credit; students compete in innovation competitions and hackathons. ✅

## ⚠ GAPS & how to obtain
IEM-specific lists of: hackathon/competition wins, named scholarship holders, student startups/founders, international exchange participants, best-project awards — **not publicly indexed.**
